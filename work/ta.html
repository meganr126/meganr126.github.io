About

I was an undergraduate teaching assistant for PLPPM 2010 & 2013, the introductory mycology class and corresponding lab. I helped prepare materials for and co-taught a weekly laboratory section.

My responsibilities as a TA included preparing and proctoring exams, grading and maintaining a grade book, and teaching my lab section. To assist with grading assignments, I created an automated assignment system in Python that the professor intends to use in coming semesters.

I received great ratings from my students and the professor of the class, earning an A+ for my role as a teaching assistant. During my mid-semester feedback meeting, neither the professor nor the administrative assistant for the class had any negative feedback for me.
