About

I'm a student at Cornell University studying Information Science with a minor in Linguistics. Extensive coursework in both Computer Science and Design allows me to approach problems from a unique, dual-sided perspective. My goal is to work and exist in the space that exists between technology and art.

Outside of class, I am an Assistant Design Editor on The Cornell Daily Sun. The Design Department creates the print paper and all graphics and visualizations needed by other departments. By working with the Editorial Board and Board of Directors, I have helped transition the Sun to Adobe inDesign and a more standardized workflow.
