Color Bop

A fun way to engage with your music.

About

This project was built for a simple assignment: design a piece of technology that interacts with your world. Color Bop is straightforward: when you play a song, it reacts.

Color Bop is easy to use: the user just has to select a song. The app contains links to several other popular music apps, so the user can choose a song from a pop-up within Color Bop.

Once a song is selected, Color Bop begins. The background colors change, cycling through a pleasing gradient of bright, bold colors with the beat of the song. Additionally, the equalizer symbols change with the volume of the song.

Most people listen to some kind of music; it is one of the best ways to relate to the world. Now, technology can relate to music.
